歡迎來到BLÜME ！

這裡是一個很特別的花朵博物館，但你看到的花並不是來自田野或花店，而是來自你的內心。

BLÜME 會透過問一些小問題跟你互動，當你真誠回答並送出後，它就會開始分析你的情緒，然後為你生成一朵專屬的情緒之花。

現在就一起來創造屬於你的情緒花園！

Welcome to BLÜME!

This is a very special flower museum, but the flowers you'll see here aren't from fields or flower shops—they come straight from your heart.

BLÜME interacts with you by asking fun little questions. When you sincerely respond and submit your answers, it analyzes your emotions and creates a unique emotional flower just for you.

This small project aims to explore the invisible emotions hidden behind words, connecting them with beautiful flowers and their meanings. The language of flowers isn't just about expressing love—it can be redefined to become a carrier of emotions, making unseen feelings visible, understood, and shared.

Let's start building your emotional garden together now!
